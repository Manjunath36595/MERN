About Our College

Annamacharya University in Rajampet, Andhra Pradesh, established in 1998 (formerly Annamacharya Institute of Technology and Sciences - AITS), is a private institution accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade and approved by AICTE. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in Engineering, Management, Science, and Technology with a strong focus on technical education, research, and placements.

Key Aspects of Annamacharya University

  • Establishment & Status: Founded in 1998 as Annamacharya Institute of Technology and Sciences (AITS), Rajampet, and later established as Annamacharya University.
  • Location: Situated on the Hyderabad–Chennai Highway near Tallapaka, the birthplace of the saint-poet Annamacharya. The campus spreads across 30 acres.
  • Accreditations & Ranking: Accredited by NAAC with an 'A+' grade and by the NBA. In 2024, its B.Tech program was ranked among the top engineering colleges by Outlook.
  • Academics: The university offers programs such as B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, MCA, B.Sc., BPT, M.Sc., and Ph.D. in fields like Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Placements & Training: The Training and Placement Cell provides industry training and soft skills development. Top recruiters include Infosys, Wipro, Tech Mahindra, Hyundai Motors, and Cognizant.
  • Campus Facilities: The campus includes modern laboratories, a digital library, Wi-Fi connectivity, and hostel facilities.
